Important Logic (Very Important)

Schedule Control

Schedule Management (Staff Only)

Staff must be able to control the clinic schedule shown to patients on the booking page.

Staff can edit:

Clinic operating days

Opening and closing hours

Special schedules (e.g., half-day schedules)

Temporarily close the clinic for specific dates if needed

Example Display on Patient Website

The information edited by staff should automatically update the patient website schedule section.

Staff Editing Capabilities

Staff must be able to:

Edit Clinic Hours

Change opening time

Change closing time

Add or remove working days

Update Clinic Information

Clinic address

Contact number

Control Appointment Availability

Enable or disable specific days

Close booking for holidays or special events

Adjust time slots for appointments

Example:
If staff changes:

Mon–Fri: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

The patient booking system must automatically update the available appointment times.

System Behavior

If clinic hours are changed, the patient booking system must follow the updated schedule.

Patients should only see available time slots within the clinic hours set by staff.

If the clinic is closed on a day, patients cannot book appointments on that day.
